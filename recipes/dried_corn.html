Cope's Creamed Corn

Cope's creamed corn is made from the sweetest corn
that is then dried and stewed with cream, butter and milk

Ingredients

  • 1 (7.5 oz) package Cope's dried corn
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 Tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt (less or more to taste)
  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • Fresh ground black pepper

Method

  1. In a large bowl, add dried corn, heavy cream, and milk. Cover and store in refrigerator overnight.
  2. After corn has soaked, transfer the corn to a large sauce pan.
  3. Add butter, salt, and sugar.
  4. Bring to a boil stirring often, then reduce heat to simmer stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes until thickened.
  5. Season to taste with salt and fresh ground pepper. Serve hot or keep over low heat until ready.
