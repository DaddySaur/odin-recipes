Brussels Sprouts With Bacon

Brussel Sprouts just like Grandma used to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs fresh Brussels Sprouts (trimmed)
  • 6 slices bacon cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 medium red onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees. Grease a large baking sheet
  2. Mix everything except the pine nuts in a large mixing bowl
    then spread evenly onto the greased baking sheet.
  3. Roast in the oven until bacon is crispy. About 40 minutes, stirring half way though.
  4. Remove from oven and toss in pine nuts before serving.
