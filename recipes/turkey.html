A Simple Roast Turkey Recipe

Turkey is great for any occasion especially when it is as simple as 5 ingredients and a few hours to spare.

Ingredients

  • 1 (18 lbs) whole turkey
  • 8 cups preferred stuffing
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened at room temp)
  • salt and fresh ground pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 quarts turkey stock (divided)

Directions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 325 degrees with rack on the lowest position of the oven.
  2. Remove neck and giblets from inside the turkey. Rinse the turkey inside and out then dry off with paper towels.
  3. Put the turkey breast side up in roasting pan and loosely fill with your choice of stuffing.
    Rub skin with butter and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour 2 cups of turkey stock into roasting pan and loosely cover with aluminum foil.
  5. Once oven is pre-heated, put put turkey in and cook for 2 1/2 hours while basting every 30 minutes.
    When the liquid has evaporated, add remaining stock to the pan.
  6. After 2 1/2 hours, remove foil and continue roasting until meat thermometer reads 165 degrees F. (about 1 1/2 more hours)
  7. When turkey has reached 165 degrees, remove turkey from the oven and transfer to a large platter to rest for 30 minutes before carving.
